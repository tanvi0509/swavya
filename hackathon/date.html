My Diet Plan

Stay healthy, eat well!

Start your day with a nutritious breakfast. Consider options like oatmeal, yogurt with fruits, or whole-grain toast.

For lunch, focus on a balanced meal. Include lean proteins, vegetables, and whole grains. Avoid excessive amounts of processed foods.

Keep your dinner light. Opt for grilled chicken or fish, along with plenty of vegetables. Avoid heavy, late-night meals.

Choose healthy snacks like nuts, fruits, or yogurt between meals to keep your energy levels stable.
